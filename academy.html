GOMUN Academy

The GOMUN Academy is a structured online training program for beginners, designed to prepare delegates for high-quality Model United Nations conferences.

  • Duration: 7–10 days
  • Format: Online live & recorded sessions
  • Who: Beginners / first-time delegates
  • Outcome: Certificate of Completion
Level 1: UN system, MUN roles, rules of procedure, public speaking
Level 2: Resolution writing, lobbying, bloc formation
Level 3: Security Council procedure & advanced debate
